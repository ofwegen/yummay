Our Story

Fire, flour,
& passion

There is something magical about watching dough transform in a wood-fired oven. At Yummay Pizza, we honor the centuries-old Neapolitan tradition -- hand-stretching every base, sourcing the finest San Marzano tomatoes, and letting the fire do what it does best.

Our charcoal and wood-fired oven gives each pizza that unmistakable smoky char, bubbling crust, and soul-warming aroma. Located at Mae Ramphueng Beach under The Royal Rayong, we are where the sand meets the sauce.

Pull up a chair, breathe in the sea air, and let us bring you a slice of Italy on the Thai coast.
